Qualtrics Custom OAS Linter

Use this linter to validate your OpenAPI specification (OAS).

This tool extends the Spectral linter with custom rulesets specific to Qualtrics public APIs.

See the Rules page for more information on the linter and the custom rulesets.

Linting your document:

Option 1: Upload your OAS in .json, .yml, or.yaml format.

Option 2 Paste the contents of your OAS file in the text area.

Select Lint Spec to lint your file. The results are displayed in the text area.
